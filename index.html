Recent Projects

Each of our projects is very special for us. You can learn about some of them below.

RETAIL

Centennial Mall

The 7 Studio worked on design renovations to refresh the Centennial Mall. Our goals were to increase its accessibility, add more functionality with the help of modern technologies. The redesigned mall now has a few green spaces and improved lighting.

HEALTHCARE

Rehab Center

In 2021, the 7 Studio was hired to assist in designing their new rehabilitation center for seniors. This new, three-story building is approximately 60,000 sq ft and includes 60 private rooms organized specifically to meet any rehabilitation needs.

EDUCATIONAL

Public Library

Our company has been hired to develop a master plan to redesign a public library without compromising the historic significance. We have considered such crucial factors as topography and proximity to downtown and schools.

EDUCATIONAL

1101 University

This renovation of a historical fraternity building into an extraordinary student habitation was just a small part of the constant refreshment of the Hill District University, neighboring the campus of Colorado University.
